<div data-max-size="40" data-min-size="14" class="fr_content fr-fc-light w-richtext" style="line-height: 26px;"><p>Kwathi kwathi ngaminazana ithile,kwichibana elinzulu elibityileyo,isele elalonwabile lenza ikhaya Lalo.Lendawana incinci ikuphela kwendawo elisele laliyazi. Lili xhuma xhuma, liqubha kwamanzi apholileyo,luzidlalela nezihlobo Lazo, unonkala neentlanzi. Lalicinga ukuba liphila obona bomi.</p><p>Ngenyi imini efudumeleyo, kwafika ufudo olukhulu ehlathini. Luhambele kude kulwandle lwasentshona, ndawo le yayinkulu, indzulu kwaye enendidi zezinto ezimangalisayo.</p><p>Lathi ufudo lokuva isele ludlala,la jonga echibini. Isele lathi lokubona olundwendwe lakhwaza “Molo mhlobo! ubomibam kwelichibi bumnandi ukufa. ndiya xhuma, ndiqubhe, ndidlale imini yonke. Ndibona yonke into kwaye ndim ophetheyo! kutheni ungangeni uzokonwaba?”</p><p>Ngenxa yomdla,lwazama ukungena echibini ufudo, kodwa hayi khona! ichibi lwaluluncinci kakhulu!wangaba zama ukuzinyakathisa waxinga.Ukuyiqonda kwalo ukuba akwenzeki lwabuya umva waqaluku balisa ngekhaya lwalo entshona langa.</p><p>“iKhaya lam, lukhulu, ligqhithela ngapha kokuba amehlo ebona. Lindzulu, akekho umntu owakhe wafikela ezantsi. Intshona langa inamaza amakhulu ukogqhitha imithi yalapha, lunendidi zezidalwa. Ezinye zigqhamile zimibalabala, ezinyeazigqibeki kodwa zonke ziyamangaza. Likhulu kangokuba noba kunetha iminyaka okanye akunethi lihlala lihleli”.</p><p>Enga amehlo kwisele,liqala ukuyiqonda ukuba kukho ilizwe elikhulu kunechibi. iSele lazama ubanomfanekiso nqondweni, wamaza,ubunzulu,nezizidalwa kuthetwa ngazo, yabe intsha kraca yonke lembali kulo.</p><p>Lathi luqhubekeka nehambo lalo ufudo, lashiyeka isele lithe ngcu ecaleni lwechibi. Lwabe luyiqonda ukuba elikhayana lalo lemfudumalo lugcugcwana kwilizwe elikhulu elimangalisayo,kwaye zininzi izinto elalungazange luyibone.</p><p>Lonto ke, isele lwaqonda ukuba mhlawumbi lixeaha lokulishiya ichibi likhe libone ezinye izinto, Ilizwe elikhulu lwalulindele ukumbonisa ububanzi nobuhle obukhoyo.</p></div>
