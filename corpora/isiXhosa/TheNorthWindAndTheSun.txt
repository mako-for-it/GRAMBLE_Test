<div data-max-size="40" data-min-size="14" class="fr_content fr-fc-light w-richtext" style="line-height: 26px;"><p>Kwathi neminazana ethile, umoya wase mantla neLanga zazixambulisana.</p><p>"Ndim onamandla" kutsho umoya wasemantla, ezingomba isifuba.</p><p>"Utsho?" latsho ilanga ngoncumo olufedumeleyo. "Ndicinguba ndim inamandla kunawe."</p><p>Kwathi kanye kunjalo babona umkhenkethi ehamba endleleni. EzinKintshe ngomanduba wedyasi.</p><p>Kwe qatha icebo kulamoya waseMantla. "Masikhuphisane! Okwaze ukwenza umkhenkethi akhulule idyasi, iyobanguye onamandla."</p><p>Lavuma iLanga, latsho laqala ukhuphiswano. Waqala umoya waseMantla, wakhokha amandla wavuthela kanobomi. Wavuthuza, wavunguza umoya ujikeleza umkhenkethi, uzama ukukrazula ibhatyi. Wanga vuthuza umoya waqiniseka ukuxhasa ibhatyi umkhenkethi ezama ukuzifudumeza.</p><p>Wavuthela, wavunguza umoya waseMantla, kodwa umkhenkethi waxhasa idyasi yakhe. Ekugqibeleni, umoya waseMantla wancama ephelelwe ngumoya.</p><p>"Yheke, lithuba lam kengoku" latsho iLanga lancuma, laqalisa ukugqatsa. Laqhakaza ilitha lelanqa lafika kumkhenkethi. Lathi ligqatsa ilanga, umkhenkethi wosula ibunzi esiva shushu. Ekugqibeleni, wakhulula idyasi wayithi tyu egcangweni Kwakhe.</p><p>Wathyafa umoya waseMantla wanikezela. "Uphumelele." Lancuma iLanga lathi "Ngelinye ixesha, ububele nemfudumalo onamandla kunonyanzelo."</p></div>
